# 👋 Welcome to the RIoT Community!

We're excited to have you here! This is the place to connect with other RIoT users and contributors, share ideas, ask questions, and help shape the future of the project.

## 🎯 What is RIoT?

RIoT is a Go-based project built with industry best practices, featuring comprehensive CI/CD pipelines, automated releases, and security scanning. Whether you're here to use RIoT, contribute to its development, or just curious about the project, you're in the right place!

## 💬 How to Use Discussions

We've organized discussions into several categories to help keep conversations focused:

- **📢 Announcements** - Stay updated with the latest news, releases, and important updates
- **💡 Ideas** - Share feature requests and suggestions for improving RIoT
- **❓ Q&A** - Ask questions and get help from the community
- **🎉 Show and Tell** - Share what you've built with RIoT
- **💭 General** - Everything else - chat, introduce yourself, or start a conversation

## 🤝 Community Guidelines

To keep our community welcoming and productive:

- **Be respectful** - Treat everyone with kindness and respect
- **Stay on topic** - Keep discussions relevant to RIoT
- **Search first** - Check if your question has already been answered
- **Be helpful** - Share your knowledge and help others when you can
- **Follow our [Code of Conduct](../blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)**

## 🐛 Bug Reports vs Discussions

- **Use Discussions** for questions, ideas, and general conversation
- **Use Issues** for confirmed bugs, feature requests with detailed specifications, and actionable tasks

If you're not sure whether something is a bug, start with a discussion!
